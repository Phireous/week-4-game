Mystery Box Game

Instructions

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.
There will be four mystery boxes below. By clicking on a box you will add a specific amount of points to your total.
You win the game by matching your total to the random target number, you lose the game if your total goes over the target number.
The value contained in each box is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the value in the boxes change. Good luck!
